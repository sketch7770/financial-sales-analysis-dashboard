Sales, Profit & Customer Segment Analysis Dashboard

This project focuses on analyzing sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and discount impact using Excel and Power BI. The dashboard helps identify high-performing categories, profitable regions, customer segment contribution, seasonal sales trends, and the relationship between discounts and profit.

The objective of the project is to convert raw business sales data into actionable insights that support better business decision-making.

Tools & Technologies Used:
Microsoft Excel
Power BI
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

Key Business Insights
California generated the highest sales and profit.
Technology category showed the strongest profitability.
Consumer segment contributed the highest overall sales.
November and December recorded peak sales performance.
Higher discount percentages significantly reduced profit margins.
Furniture category had decent sales but relatively lower profitability.
